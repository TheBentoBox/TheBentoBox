Life

I was born and raised on the Gulf Coast of Florida. Some of my earliest memories are sitting with my dad watching him play Diablo I and Command & Conquer: Red Alert II. I've loved games ever since I had the capacity to do so.


Passions

I'm interested in game development, game design, and web development. I have a passion for creating extensible frameworks, interactive online experiences, and games which will inspire memories within the player or user.
