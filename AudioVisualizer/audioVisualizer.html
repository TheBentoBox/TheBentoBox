Customize the visualizer by selecting (clicking) on one of the colored tabs in the right visualizer panel, or one of the circles in the panel below the color palette. You can change the color of the selected object with the color palette. The color tabs can also be dragged up and down the panel to further modify the gradient. Right click a tab to delete it. The currently selected tab or circle will be indicated by the black arrow.


The background URL input needs a direct link to the image (not just the page it's on). If you put in a URL and it just looks black, try right clicking the image and clicking "Copy image URL", then pasting that in the text box.
